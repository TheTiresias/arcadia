It started, as most things do, with an ordinary morning. The light came in sideways through the blinds and drew thin lines across the floor.

She sat at the table and did not drink her coffee. She had made it out of habit, and habit had carried her this far, but no further.

---

## A Small Interruption

The phone rang. She let it ring. Outside, a car started and pulled away, and then it was quiet again — the particular quiet of a weekday, when everyone else has somewhere to be.
